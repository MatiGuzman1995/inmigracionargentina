Fees

ITA charges a total fee of $600 USD

The Argentina immigration (Residency) process is a ten-step procedure. This process requires the applicant to appear at the immigration office at least 8 times over a 2-month period with the proper documentation.

The fee that ITA charges will provide you with the following services:

  1. A team member will accompany you, all 8 times over the two months, to the immigration office and assist you with the government forms every time you are required at the immigration office.
  2. A team member will input all your residency/immigration documents, required by the Argentine government, and upload them to the immigration department’s website.
  3. A team member will accompany you to the government agency that will provide you with a CDI card. You’ll need this card to purchase a vehicle, open a bank account, or buy real estate.
  4. A team member will accompany you to the bank to assist you in opening an account.
  5. A team member will be with you through every step to ensure you receive your residency.

Government immigration fees

Government immigration fees can vary depending on your type of residency and your country of origin. It is advisable to budget approximately $250 USD for one person and $450 USD for a family of four.

These are the total fees you will be required to pay in order to obtain temporary residency lasting two years, at which time you will be eligible to apply for permanent residency.
